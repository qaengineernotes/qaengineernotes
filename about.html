About Me

Hi there! I'm Nikunj Mistri, a passionate QA Engineer with 5+ years of experience in ensuring software quality and implementing test automation solutions.

Welcome to QA Blogs

Your go-to resource for all things related to quality assurance, software testing, and automation engineering. This blog is dedicated to sharing knowledge, insights, and practical tips that empower testers, developers, and QA professionals to excel in their careers and deliver high-quality software.

Who Am I?

With over 5+ years of experience in the field of software testing, I've had the privilege of working on a wide range of projects, from small-scale applications to large enterprise systems. Over the years, I've developed a deep passion for ensuring software quality and helping teams build robust, user-friendly products.

I specialize in Python-based test automation, leveraging tools like Selenium WebDriver, Pytest, and other frameworks to streamline testing processes and improve efficiency. Whether it's designing automated test scripts, debugging complex issues, or implementing best practices in QA workflows, I thrive on finding innovative solutions to challenging problems.

In addition to automation, I bring extensive expertise in manual testing and a strong foundation in test planning, strategy development, and defect lifecycle management. My experience spans across various testing types, including regression testing, user acceptance testing (UAT), API testing, and performance testing basics.

My Expertise

Manual Testing

  • Test Planning & Strategy Development: Crafting comprehensive test plans and strategies tailored to project requirements.
  • Test Case Design & Execution: Creating detailed test cases and executing them to ensure maximum test coverage.
  • Defect Lifecycle Management: Identifying, tracking, and resolving defects efficiently using tools like JIRA and TestRail.

Automation Skills

  • CI/CD Integration: Integrating automated tests into CI/CD pipelines for faster feedback loops and continuous delivery.
